About

A comprehensive professional suite designed to assist engineers and machinists with complex mechanical calculations. Originally focused on helical gear setups, our platform has expanded to include multi-stage gear train analysis and industrial fastener torque specifications, ensuring accuracy and efficiency in the workshop and the design office.

Key Features
Specialized modules: Helical Gear Setup, Gear Train Analysis, and Bolt Torque.
High-precision algorithms providing optimal mechanical configurations and load analysis.
19-language support — from English to Arabic, Japanese, and Myanmar.
Responsive & mobile-optimized for use on any device.
How to Use
1

Access the Dashboard to select your specific engineering requirement.

2

Input your technical parameters (dimensions, ratios, or friction factors).

3

Click Calculate Gears — browse up to 50 optimal gear setups sorted by accuracy.
